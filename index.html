Toivoniemi

Toivoniemi is one of the greatest neighborhoods of Oulu. This residential area is built on an island next to Tuira, bordered by Merikoski canal. Toivoniemi is a beautiful, nice area to live in, surrounded by waterfront and garden-like environment. Toivoniemi has a population of around 600 and is very centrally located, only one kilometer away from the Oulu City Centre.
